False Ceiling interior

The innovative false ceiling system is an aesthetic and decorative finish for ceiling in private rooms. It can be used in living rooms, bathrooms, and halls. Infratop has been designed with both functionality and aesthetics in mind.

The construction of a false ceiling can be undertaken in any home, regardless of its size. Typically, this type of ceiling is installed at a minimum distance of 8 inches from the original ceiling.

So, what exactly is a false ceiling? Essentially, it’s a type of ceiling designed to fit beneath the original ceiling. Typically suspended by wooden or metal frames, a false ceiling design creates the illusion of a lower ceiling. Sometimes, portions of the original ceiling remain visible, earning it the nickname ‘dropped ceiling’.
